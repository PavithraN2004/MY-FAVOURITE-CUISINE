CAKE

INGREDIENTS
MAIDA
BAKING POWDER
COCOA POWDER
EGGS
CHOCOLATE SAUCE
POWDERED SUGAR
--------------
RECEIPE
MIX EGGS,MAIDA,COCOA POWDER,POWDERED SUGAR IN A MIXING BOWL.ADD THE MIXED BATTER IN A BAKING TRAY AND BAKE IN THE OVEN AT 180C.ONCE BAKED,TOP THE CAKE WITH CHOCOLATE SAUCE AND SERVE HOT.

PIZZA

INGREDIENTS
MAIDA
CHEESE
TOMATO
EGGS
OLIVE
YEAST
SALT
CAPSIUM
--------------
RECEIPE
MIX EGGS,MAIDA,YEAST,SALT IN A MIXING BOWL.ADD THE MIXED BATTER IN A BAKING TRAY AND BAKE IN THE OVEN AT 180C.ONCE BAKED,TOP THE BASE WITH CHOPPED TOMATOES,SHREDDED CHEESE,CHOPPED CAPSIUM AND BAKE IT ONCE AGAIN.SERVE HOT.

ORANGE JUICE

INGREDIENTS
ORANGE
WATER
ICE CUBES
POWDERED SUGAR
--------------
RECEIPE
MIX WATER AND SQUEEZED ORANGE TOGETHER UNTIL DISSOLVED.ADD POWDERED SUGAR FOR SWEETNESS.ADD ICE CUBES AND SERVE CHILL.

INGREDIENTS
WHIPPED CREAM
FULL FAT MILK
COFEE POWDER
POWDERED SUGAR
--------------
RECEIPE
MIX MILK AND COFEE POWDER.ADD THE COFFEE IN A MUG.FROTH THE COFFEE WITH A FROTHING MACHINE.ADD WHIPPED CEAM IN YOUR FAVOURITE DESIGN ON TOP OF THE COFFEE.

MUSHROOM SOUP

INGREDIENTS
SALT
WATER
CORNFLOUR
MUSHROOM
CORN
FRESH CREAM
--------------
RECEIPE
BURN THE GAS STOVE AND SAUTE CHOPPED MUSHROOMS AND CORN.PLACE THEM IN A SMALL BOWL.BOIL WATER AND ADD SOME SALT.ADD SOME DISSOLVED CORNFLOUR PASTE IN THE BOILED WATER.LET THEM BOIL FOR A WHILE AND ADD THE SAUTED MUSHROOMS,CORN AND SOME FRESH CREAM.SERVE HOT.
